NBA Stats API

Comprehensive data on NBA players and teams from 2003 to 2024

About

Welcome to the NBA Stats API! This API provides detailed information on NBA players and teams spanning the years 2003 to 2024. Whether you're a developer, statistician, or an avid basketball fan, our API offers a wealth of data to help you analyze and enjoy the game.

Features

  • Player statistics including points, rebounds, assists, and more.
  • Team performance data including win/loss records, standings, and historical data.
  • Data updated regularly to ensure accuracy and comprehensiveness.
  • Easy-to-use endpoints for quick integration into your applications.
